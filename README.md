# photography-business-analytics-dashboard

This project analyzes a photography company's business performance across Marysville, WA and surrounding cities, including Everett, Lake Stevens, and Arlington.  The dashboard provides insights into revenue, bookings, customer behavior, and photographer performance.

Built a relational database and analytics solution for a photography business operating in the Marysville, WA region.

Developed SQL queries to analyze revenue trends, booking patterns, and customer behavior across multiple service types.

Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard to track KPIs including total revenue, booking volume, and photographer performance.

Identified key business insights such as seasonal demand trends and top-performing services to support data-driven decision-making.

Business Insight

- Wedding photography generated the highest revenue across all services.
  
- Family portrait sessions had the highest booking frequency.
  
- Marysville and Everett were the top-performing locations.
  
- Peak booking activity occurred during summer months (June–August).
  
- A small group of customers contributed to a large portion of revenue.
